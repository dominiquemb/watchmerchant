FAQs

Q. I'd like to buy this item. How do I proceed?

A. On the offer page, click on the green "Show Seller" button, this will reveal the seller's details. From there click the "Contact Seller" button. This will give you options - phone number, email address or if the seller has our VIP Appintment Booking service you can book an appointment online.

Q. I would like to negotiate a price with the seller. What should I do?

Contact the seller through the steps above and speak to them about the price.
